About Us

Milena Homes is a premier residential and commercial development company dedicated to creating quality living spaces and robust commercial establishments. In addition to our development projects, we proudly operate a Property Management and Sales Division, ensuring every stage of our service meets the highest standards of excellence.

Our Vision

A Home for Every Kenyan. We believe that every Kenyan deserves a comfortable, affordable home—a space that becomes a foundation for building multigenerational wealth and a better future.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop decent, affordable residential homes and commercial establishments that serve as catalysts for sustainable economic growth. We partner with the Kenyan Government to turn the dream of affordable housing into a reality, offering homes on favorable terms that meet individual financial capacities.

Flexible Home Building Models

Our Jijengee Model allows home owners to determine the pace of home development with customizable options—from building a basic shell to creating a fully finished home with expandable features. The Lena Model supports customers in saving towards their dream home, with construction starting once the required savings are accumulated.
